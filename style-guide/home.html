JCDREAM

Joint Center for Deployment and  Research in Earth Abundant Materials

Establishing a collaborative and transformative program of research, development, deployment, and training in earth-abundant materials science, engineering, and advanced manufacturing to accelerate the development of next-generation clean energy and transportation technologies in Washington State

Goals

  • national industrial supply-chain security,
  • economic sustainability, and
  • sound environmental stewardship.

JCDREAM 1st Annual Symposium, Oct. 3-4, 2016: JCDREAM held its inaugural symposium and brought together researchers and policy makers in the area of “earth abundant materials” to discuss latest developments and trends as well as regional strategies in this area. Washington State Representative Norma Smith welcomed the participants to the symposium and Dr. Alex King, Director of the Critical Materials Hub in Ames, IA was the keynote speaker.
